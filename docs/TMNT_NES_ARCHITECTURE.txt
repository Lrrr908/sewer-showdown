  THE 10 PILLARS:

  1. TURTLE MANAGEMENT
     Each turtle is a strategic resource, not just a skin.
     Losing Donatello early = run is probably over.

  2. TWO-MODE TENSION
     Overworld is dangerous (steamrollers).
     Interiors are combat-focused.
     Neither mode is "safe."

  3. OPTIONAL EXPLORATION
     Most entrances are optional. Knowing which to skip
     and which to farm = mastery.

  4. ITEM FARMING AS GAMEPLAY
     Re-entering areas to farm pizza/scrolls is intended.
     Area 6 removes this = dread.

  5. INSTANT-KILL HAZARDS
     Steam rollers, fire pits, crushers, coral.
     Creates constant vigilance even for skilled players.

  6. ENVIRONMENTAL VARIETY
     Sewers, buildings, rooftops, underwater, conveyors,
     magnets, rope bridges â€” within the SAME area.

  7. VERTICAL LEVEL DESIGN
     Ladders, multi-floor buildings, ceiling mechanics.
     Not just left-to-right.

  8. BOSS PATTERN MASTERY
     Each boss has a specific pattern and safe strategy.
     Reward for observation, not just DPS.

  9. ESCALATING HOSTILITY
     Each area strips away more safety nets.
     Area 6 = everything you relied on is gone.

  10. THE MAP IS YOUR ONLY GUIDE
      No quest markers, no tutorials, no hand-holding.
      Pause screen map + memory = navigation.
